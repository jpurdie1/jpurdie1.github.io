Who are we?

The UNC Undergraduate Business Technology Club is a collection of diverse and talented individuals at one of the best public universities in the nation. Our focus on the intersection of business and technology attracts some of the most ambitious individuals across campus.

Why partner with us?

In today's competitive market, recruiting top talent is more important than ever to the success of your organization. We are excited to connect you with our members, some of the most accomplished individuals on campus, who are eager to learn more about various roles in the business and technology industries.
